Our Mission Statement

Our webpage for the Unix Fundamentals class at East Tennessee State University is dedicated to providing students with a comprehensive and accessible online resource. Our goal is to empower students to understand and navigate the Unix operating system effectively. Through engaging content and practical examples, we foster a hands-on learning experience, enabling students to develop proficiency in Unix fundamentals as well as scripting in a Unix-based computing environment.
